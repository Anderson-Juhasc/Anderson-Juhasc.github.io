Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services

What is the average timeframe to develop a website?

The timeframe depends on the complexity of the project. A simple institutional website may take 2 to 4 weeks, while more complex projects such as e-commerce sites or custom platforms can take 1 to 3 months.

How does the development process work?

The process includes: 1) Requirements gathering and briefing, 2) Planning and architecture, 3) Interface design, 4) Development, 5) Testing and adjustments, 6) Launch, and 7) Support. I maintain continuous communication throughout each phase to ensure expectations are met.

Do you offer maintenance services after delivery?

Yes, I offer monthly maintenance plans that include security updates, bug fixes, minor improvements, and performance monitoring.
